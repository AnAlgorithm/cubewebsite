Welcome to My site about Rubik's cubes!

The Rubik's Cube is a 3-D combination puzzle invented in 1974 by Hungarian sculptor and professor of architecture Ern≈ë Rubik. Originally called the Magic Cube, the puzzle was licensed by Rubik to be sold by Ideal Toy Corp. in 1980 via businessman Tibor Laczi and Seven Towns founder Tom Kremer. Rubik's Cube won the 1980 German Game of the Year special award for Best Puzzle. As of January 2009, 350 million cubes had been sold worldwide, making it the world's bestselling puzzle game and bestselling toy.

Types of Cubes

  1. Rubik's Cubes

    The original Rubik's cubes were not so advanced. They were hard to turn, and frequently used to get stuck. While it is okay for a beginner, these types of cubes are not used for timed solves.

  2. Speed Cubes

    Speed cubes are cubes designed for Cubing competitions, and are very smooth. They come black-stickered, white-stickered, stickerless and carbon-fiber, and range in sizes. Many companies have many cubes with different technology for advanced and smooth turning.

  3. Smart Cubes

    Smart cubes have advanced technology embedded in cubes, that can track and monitor the user's movements. Most can be connected to apps, and give a real-time status of the cube. They are bluetooth connected. The GoCube, GAN 356i, Rubik's connected and X-Mars cube are examples.
